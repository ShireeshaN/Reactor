What is a Stainless Steel Reactor?

Stainless Steel Reactors are robust process vessels designed for mixing, heating, cooling and chemical reactions under controlled conditions. Manufactured using SS 304 / SS 316 / SS 316L, they offer excellent mechanical strength, hygiene and long operational life.

Types of Stainless Steel Reactors

Jacketed Reactor

Precise heating & cooling using steam, hot oil or chilled water.

Pressure Reactor

Designed for high-pressure chemical reactions.

Vacuum Reactor

Ideal for solvent recovery & low-temperature reactions.

Agitated Reactor

Uniform mixing using anchor, turbine or paddle agitators.
