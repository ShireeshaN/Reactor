Single-Use Reactors

Disposable bioreactor systems designed for sterile operation, rapid setup, and flexible production in biopharmaceutical and biotechnology processes.

What is a Single-Use Reactor?

Single-Use Reactors are disposable bioreactor systems widely used in biopharmaceutical and biotechnology industries. They eliminate cleaning, reduce downtime, and ensure contamination-free production.

Key Advantages

Sterile Processing

Pre-sterilized systems ensure zero cross-contamination.

No CIP / SIP

Eliminates complex cleaning and sterilization cycles.

Rapid Scale-Up

Quick deployment for multi-product facilities.

Cost Efficient

Lower water, energy and labor consumption.

Applications

Vaccine Manufacturing

Fast and sterile production of viral and recombinant vaccines.

Cell & Gene Therapy

Low-shear environment ideal for sensitive cell cultures.

Biopharmaceuticals

Suitable for monoclonal antibodies and protein expression.

R&D Laboratories

Ideal for pilot-scale trials and process optimization.
