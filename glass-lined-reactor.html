Glass-Lined Reactors

Glass-lined reactors are high-performance process vessels that combine the mechanical strength of carbon steel with the exceptional chemical resistance of a glass lining. This construction makes them ideal for handling corrosive, aggressive, and high-purity chemical reactions.

They are extensively used in pharmaceutical, chemical, agrochemical, and specialty industries where contamination control, safety, and durability are mission-critical.

Types of Glass-Lined Reactors

Batch Processing

Standard Glass-Lined Reactor

Ideal for pharmaceutical and chemical batch reactions ensuring corrosion resistance and product purity.

Thermal Control

Jacketed Glass-Lined Reactor

Engineered for precise temperature regulation using heating or cooling media across reaction cycles.

High Heat Transfer

Limpet / Half-Coil Reactor

Designed for viscous, exothermic reactions requiring rapid and uniform heat dissipation.

Pressure Rated

Pressure & Vacuum Reactor

Built for safe operation under high pressure and deep vacuum conditions.

Advanced Mixing

Agitated Glass-Lined Reactor

Uniform mixing with anchor, turbine, or retreat-curve agitators.

R&D Scale

Pilot / Lab Scale Reactor

Optimized for process development, scale-up trials, and research applications.
