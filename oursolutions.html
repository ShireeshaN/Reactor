Our Process

How We Execute Reactor Lifecycle Solutions

At Reactor Care, every project follows a structured technical methodology designed to ensure safety, precision, and long-term reliability.

Professional execution is as important as technical expertise.

01

Technical Consultation & Requirement Understanding

  • Reactor type (GLR / SS)
  • Process application
  • Operating temperature & pressure
  • Chemical exposure conditions
  • Existing issue or breakdown
  • Plant shutdown timeline
  • Compliance sensitivity

This allows us to assess risk level and urgency.

02

On-Site Inspection & Condition Assessment

  • Glass lining spark testing (GLR)
  • Thickness measurement
  • Internal visual inspection
  • Shaft alignment measurement
  • Vibration analysis
  • Mechanical seal inspection
  • Jacket integrity check
  • Structural inspection

Assessment Report Includes:

  • Immediate risk areas
  • Progressive damage
  • Potential failure zones
  • Safety concerns
03

Root Cause Analysis

  • Thermal shock damage patterns
  • Corrosion mapping
  • Shaft misalignment sources
  • Seal degradation reasons
  • Pressure fatigue indicators
  • Operational overload factors

We eliminate recurring failure, not just symptoms.

04

Engineering Solution Planning

  • Repair strategy
  • Replacement plan
  • Refurbishment approach
  • Re-glassing schedule
  • Component upgrade recommendation
  • Shutdown duration estimate

Focus: Minimum downtime • Cost efficiency • Long-term stability

05

Controlled Execution

  • Safety protocol compliance
  • Technical supervision
  • Proper dismantling procedure
  • Correct torque application
  • Precision alignment
  • Component compatibility check

Strict quality control at every stage.

06

Testing & Validation

  • Spark testing (GLR)
  • Pressure testing
  • Hydro testing
  • Alignment re-verification
  • Vibration check
  • Seal leakage testing

Only after validation do we clear operation.

07

Documentation & Reporting

  • Inspection report
  • Damage mapping report
  • Replacement details
  • Test results
  • Maintenance recommendations
  • Future inspection schedule

Documentation builds long-term reliability planning.

08

Post-Service Support

  • Follow-up technical support
  • Maintenance scheduling assistance
  • Aftermarket spare support
  • Emergency response availability
