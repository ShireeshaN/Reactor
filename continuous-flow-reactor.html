Continuous Flow Reactors

Modern, modular, and flexible flow chemistry systems engineered for precise, scalable, and safe chemical reactions.

Batch Chemistry

Traditional method where reagents are loaded, mixed, reacted, and collected as a single batch.

Continuous Flow Chemistry

Reagents flow continuously through the reactor, reacting in real time with uninterrupted product collection.

Types of Continuous Flow Reactors

Chip-based Reactors

Microreactors offering high surface-area-to-volume ratio for precise reaction and temperature control.

Coil-based Reactors

Tubular flow reactors made from PTFE, PFA, or stainless steel, widely used due to simplicity and cost-effectiveness.

Packed Bed Reactors

Designed for heterogeneous reactions where solid catalysts or reagents are packed into columns or cartridges.

Flow Chemistry Fundamentals

Flow chemistry is all about precise control of reaction parameters: time, temperature, mixing, and stoichiometry. Our systems allow you to:

  • Control addition and mixing
  • Control stoichiometric equivalents
  • Control reaction times
  • Control reaction temperatures

Benefits of Flow Chemistry

Faster Reactions

Increased reaction rates through pressurization, efficient heat transfer, and superior mixing.

Safer Reactions

Enhanced safety by minimizing the quantity of hazardous intermediates in the system.

Faster Optimization

Rapid parameter screening and automated optimization of temperature, pressure, and residence time.

Easy Scale-Up

Linear scaling from laboratory to production without complex re-development steps.
